How AI Functions

- Data Processing: AI uses vast amounts of data to learn and identify patterns. - Machine Learning (ML): A subset of AI that allows systems to learn and improve from experience. - Neural Networks: Modeled after the human brain, neural networks help AI in recognizing patterns and making predictions. - Deep Learning: A more advanced form of neural networks used for complex tasks like image recognition and natural language processing (NLP).

The Science Behind ChatGPT

  1. Natural Language Processing (NLP): ChatGPT uses NLP to understand human language and generate meaningful responses.
  2. Transformer Architecture: GPT uses a transformer model, allowing it to understand the context of words in relation to one another.
  3. Pre-training & Fine-tuning: The model is pre-trained on a vast corpus of data and fine-tuned to generate human-like responses.
